Snatch Jack

                            
Snatch Jack is a card game based on a very popular Indian game known as “Gulam Chor”. 

                            How to Play:
                            

                            The game is played with standard deck of cards only difference is one Jack is taken out of deck. Once the cards are dealt to each player, players get turns to snatch a card from other players, discarding any pairs they have until someone is left with the unpaid jack card. The player who holds the jack is thief.
