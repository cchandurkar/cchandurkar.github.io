                    What is InstaVocab?
                

                  

                  

                    

                      
                      

                      

                      
                        
                      
                      

                      

                    

                    

                      
InstaVocab is an android app designed and develope for GRE and GMAT aspirants. It lets you build vocabulary required for exam quickly. It also provides you rich set of features that helps you remember and quickly access difficult words.

                      

                      Features:
                      
    
                         
  1. All Words (3600+)
    2. 
                         
  2. Barron's High Frequency Word List
    3. 
                         
  3. Kaplan's High Frequency Word List
    4. 
                         
  4. Manhattan's High Frequency Word List
    5. 
                         
  5. Learn to pronounce word accurately
    6. 
                         
  6. Mark difficulty words for quick access
    7. 
                         
  7. Test your vocab
    8. 
                         
  8. Search words, meanings, sentences
    9. 
                         
  9. Free and No Advertising
    10.

When?

                  

                  

                    

                      
Writing this update from scratch is big thing. I'm trying hard to find a time from my masters studies. Honestly, I don't know. It might take a week or months.  Designing, prototyping, testing, fixing bugs and then marketting takes time and I am working on it all alone.

                    

                  

                  
Ads and Revenue?

                  

                    

                      
Not Yet !! My moto behind writing this app was to help out the needies and it's working out pretty well I guess. I consider InstaVocab as a small contribution to education society and I want to keep it free. In-app advertisements are irritating and I hate them myself. I feel it punches holes in expected user experience. However, new update MIGHT have a donate option but I'm not sure about putting it there yet.
